About Us

Established in 2005

Frontop has been consistently providing top quality service for over a decade, and is now one of the top engineering consulting firms in Ontario. We focus on geotechnical instrumentation and monitoring, with over 50 employees including Professional Engineers, Instrumentation and Monitoring specialists, Software Engineers and Noise and Vibration experts.

Providing geotechnical engineering services in Ontario, Canada

Frontop provides multiple engineering services including: geotechnical instrumentation and monitoring, real-time noise and vibration monitoring, computer science and software developing engineering, precision surveying and construction layout, geotechnical engineering, as well as structural, civil work, electrical and mechanical (HVAC, plumbing and fire protection) design. We incorporate our wide range of knowledge and experience to ensure works of excellence and high quality, guaranteeing that the needs of our clients are satisfied..
