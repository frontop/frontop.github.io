Geotechnical Monitoring

Category: | website: https://frontop.ca

As our primary form of service, Geotechnical Instrumentation and Monitoring is Frontop’s main area of expertise. Our years of experience and knowledge guarantees that our clients receive accurate and high-quality work. We have shown strong monitoring performance on our previous projects for TTC subway tunnels, subway stations, Metrolinx, CN Railways grade seperation and many other government infrastructures. Frontop’s extensive monitoring program that includes design, instrument installation, data collection, reporting and real time web-based data access, ensures that the client’s needs are fully satisfied. Some of our geotechnical instrumentation and monitoring services include:

Inclinometers Crack Gauges Open Standpipe Piezometers Vibrating Wire Piezometers Vibrating Wire Strain Gauges Load Cells Tape Extensometers Multi-Point Borehole Extensometers Tiltmeters and Displace Transducers Pre & Post Construction Condition Survey
